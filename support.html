How to Play

  • You're shown a target time (for example, 12 seconds).
  • Press START — the timer begins, but it's hidden.
  • When you feel the target time has passed, press STOP.
  • The closer you are, the higher your rank and score.

Frequently Asked

Is my progress saved? Yes — everything is stored locally on your device. Reinstalling or deleting the app will clear it. You can back up or move your progress with Export Save in Settings.

Does TempoGuess need the internet? No. It works fully offline.

How do reminders work? They're optional local notifications. Toggle them in Settings, or in your device's system settings.

How do I reset my progress? Settings → Reset All Progress.
